Calibre Community

At Calibre, we are such a small team that we rely heavily on our community to inspire us, educate us, and help us grow.

Good referrals are a talent, not a skill. It takes a special kind of creative energy to connect the dots and see how we're a good fit for someone. The reality is that you probably do all that quite intuitively, but that doesn't mean we appreciate you any less.

Because of this, we offer a referral fee for business that you send our way, and you can see the rates we're offering below. We also recognise that some people would prefer not to receive a fee for referrals because it keeps the introduction pure, makes it feel easier to ask us for a favour in future, and simplifies the relationship.

Referral fee - 5%

As standard, we offer 5% of the total project budget for a referral or introduction to a client. The total project budget is counted as the first three months of work with that client, and any new scopes that kick off after that 3 months are considered to be earned on our own merit.

Ambassador fee - 10%

For serial referrers that very much understand who we work best with, help us understand what the clients' real business goals are, or position us as the preferred or only contractor being considered for a project that must go ahead, we offer a 10% fee instead.
