DeepMind is one of the worlds leading (and most secretive) AI organisations in the world, and is likely to play a significant role in the future of humanity. Calibre's creative team was involved to explore the scientific relevance of AI into the past and the future as we contributed towards an internal corp comms initiative.

Berenberg

The worlds oldest merchant bank is living up to its legacy as it investigates Digital as a way to stay competative into the future. Calibre collaborates with Berenberg's Digital Transformation & Innovation initiatives by taking responsibity for everything from Product Strategy down to Frontend Development using React.js.

Rockpool Investments

We rebranded the companys logo, offices, print & digital assets, but the Gutenberg powered Wordpress theme we made them was ahead of its time. Their bespoke page editor is as dynamic as the company itself and every time we load the website we spot a change they've made without having to pick up the phone to ask our help.

Subutai

This cyber startup's impressive product scans the millions of devices in an enterprise to reveal their cyber risk profile. Calibre was fist involved as product consultants, where we campaigned for a new architecture that sped up development by at least 5x. We later built their website at esc.co.
