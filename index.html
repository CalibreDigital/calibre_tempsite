Calibre Culture

Our mission is to make the cutting edge of digital product design as accessible as possible to our partners.

Considering how fast the landscape of tools, workflows, and design patterns are changing, we've built a culture around learning, and we're never going to stop sharpening our axe.

Calibre Community

We find that the best way to stay on the cutting edge of digital is by being plugged into a network of brilliant entrepreneurs, contractors, and mentors.

We've learnt from remote teams like Zapier, Buffer, and freelance collectives how to get a new contributor up to speed in a flash. That means seamless onboarding, documented design decisions, and communication that can't be misunderstood.
