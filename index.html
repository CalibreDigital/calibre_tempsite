We are Calibre

When consultants want to roll up their sleeves

we create a space for brilliant people to put on their tech hats and get things done

Calibre is a Digital Product Studio with a culture of optimisation. We achieve that through three pillars:

Communication - Tools - Context

Communication

All business mistakes could be boiled down to communication. This perspective gives us huge potential to deliver more value, with a higher success rate. Our biggest inspiration so far has been from the Remote Working movement and Decentralised Organisations, where impeccable communication is a skill learned by every contributor.

Tools

With the right tools, we can achieve a days' work in several keystrokes. We've used most of the digital production tools in the markets, which makes us one of the rare studios that can swiftly migrate a site from Bespoke code to Wordpress, integrate with a Shopify e-commerce subsite, and sprinkle a flair of animation across your whole digital identity.

Context

To us, Context means understanding the "why" behind anything you do. Context enables every contributor to consider a better approach before embarking on a job, which great no matter how you look at it: Everyone has the opportunity to bring their inner genius to work, things get done faster, and our products can be more innovative.
